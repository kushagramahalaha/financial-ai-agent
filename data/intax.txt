[INCOME_TAX_BASICS_INDIA]

Income tax is a direct tax imposed on individuals based on income.

Types of Income:
- Salary
- Business/Profession
- Capital Gains
- House Property
- Other Sources

---

[TAX_REGIMES]

1. Old Tax Regime:
- Allows deductions
- Sections: 80C, 80D, HRA, etc.

2. New Tax Regime:
- Lower tax rates
- No major deductions

---

[TAX_SLABS_NEW_REGIME]

Income Range (₹):
- 0 – 3L → 0%
- 3L – 6L → 5%
- 6L – 9L → 10%
- 9L – 12L → 15%
- 12L – 15L → 20%
- Above 15L → 30%

---

[DEDUCTIONS_OLD_REGIME]

1. Section 80C:
- Limit: ₹1.5 lakh
- Includes: PPF, ELSS, LIC, FD

2. Section 80D:
- Health insurance premium

3. HRA:
- House rent allowance exemption

---

[CAPITAL_GAINS_TAX]

1. Short-Term Capital Gain (STCG):
- Holding < 1 year (stocks)
- Tax: 15%

2. Long-Term Capital Gain (LTCG):
- Holding > 1 year
- Tax: 10% above ₹1 lakh

---

[TAX_SAVING_TIPS]

- Invest in ELSS for tax + returns
- Use 80C fully
- Choose regime based on deductions

---

[EXAMPLE]

If LTCG = ₹1.5 lakh:
Taxable = ₹50,000
Tax = 10% of ₹50,000 = ₹5,000
