[PORTFOLIO_BASICS]

A portfolio is a collection of financial investments such as stocks, mutual funds, bonds, and cash.

Goal:
- Maximize returns
- Minimize risk through diversification

---

[ASSET_CLASSES]

1. Equity:
- Stocks, equity mutual funds
- High risk, high return

2. Debt:
- Bonds, fixed deposits, debt funds
- Low risk, stable returns

3. Gold:
- Hedge against inflation

4. Cash:
- Liquidity and emergency use

---

[DIVERSIFICATION]

Diversification reduces risk by spreading investments across different assets.

Example:
- Stocks + Bonds + Gold

Avoid:
- Investing all money in one stock or sector

---

[RISK_PROFILING]

1. Conservative Investor:
- Low risk tolerance
- Focus: Capital protection
- Allocation: 20% equity, 60% debt, 20% gold

2. Moderate Investor:
- Balanced risk
- Allocation: 50% equity, 30% debt, 20% gold

3. Aggressive Investor:
- High risk tolerance
- Allocation: 70–80% equity, rest debt/gold

---

[ASSET_ALLOCATION_STRATEGY]

Rule:
Asset Allocation = Based on age + risk appetite

Example Rule:
Equity % ≈ 100 - Age

Example:
Age = 25 → Equity ≈ 75%

---

[REBALANCING]

Rebalancing means adjusting portfolio to maintain target allocation.

When to rebalance:
- Every 6–12 months
- When allocation deviates by >5–10%

Example:
Target equity = 60%
Actual = 75% → Sell equity, buy debt

---

[INVESTMENT_STRATEGIES]

1. Long-Term Investing:
- Hold assets for years
- Focus on compounding

2. Value Investing:
- Buy undervalued assets

3. Growth Investing:
- Invest in high-growth companies

4. Passive Investing:
- Index funds (NIFTY 50)

---

[GOAL_BASED_INVESTING]

Each investment should have a goal:

- Short-Term (0–3 years):
  → Debt funds, FD

- Medium-Term (3–7 years):
  → Hybrid funds

- Long-Term (7+ years):
  → Equity, index funds

---

[EMERGENCY_FUND]

Maintain:
- 3–6 months of expenses

Keep in:
- Savings account or liquid funds

---

[COMMON_MISTAKES]

- No diversification
- Frequent buying/selling
- Ignoring asset allocation
- Investing without goals
- Emotional decisions

---

[EXAMPLE_PORTFOLIO]

Investor: Age 25, Moderate Risk

Allocation:
- 60% Equity (Index + Large Cap)
- 25% Debt Funds
- 10% Gold
- 5% Cash

---

[DECISION_RULES_FOR_AGENT]

- If user is young (<30): Suggest higher equity exposure
- If risk is low: Increase debt allocation
- If goal is short-term: Avoid equity
- If market is volatile: Suggest SIP instead of lump sum
- If portfolio is concentrated: Recommend diversification
